About US

Mindset is a software company that provides ecommerce platforms for brands to optimize their customer experiences. We believe that great experiences define great brands. Our mission is to empower brands to build and optimize ecommerce experiences for their customers — creating brand loyalty and business growth.
Mindset was founded in 2014 by entrepreneurs who saw an opportunity to provide a better ecommerce experience for brands and their customers. We are a team of engineers, designers, and marketers who are passionate about building great software and delivering amazing customer experiences.
We are headquartered in San Francisco, CA and have offices in New York City and Seattle, WA. Great experiences define great brands, At Mindset, our mission is to empower brands to build and optimize ecommerce experiences for their customers — creating brand loyalty and business growth.
